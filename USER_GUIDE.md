# EAR Shyogwe CMS - User Manual

## 1. Getting Started

### Login
- Navigate to the login page.
- Enter your **Email** and **Password**.
- Click **Log in**.
- *Note: If you forgot your password, contact an Administrator (Boss).*

### Your Dashboard
- **Top Cards:** Quick summary of Total Givings, Expenses, and Net Balance.
- **Charts:** Visual trends of your financial and attendance data.
- **Sidebar:** Navigation to all main sections (Givings, Expenses, Attendance, etc.).

---

## 2. Finance Management

### Recording a Giving (Income)
1. Go to **Givings** in the sidebar.
2. Click **+ Record New Giving**.
3. Select the **Church** (if you are an Archid/Admin).
4. Select **Giving Type** (e.g., Tithe, Offering).
5. Enter **Amount** and **Date**.
6. Click **Save**.

### Recording an Expense
1. Go to **Expenses** in the sidebar.
2. Click **+ Record New Expense**.
3. Select **Category** (e.g., Construction, Salary).
4. Enter **Amount**, **Date**, and a brief **Description**.
5. (Optional) Upload a **Receipt** image/PDF.
6. Click **Save**.
7. *Note: Large expenses may trigger a notification to the Diocese for approval.*

### Exporting Reports
- On any list page (Givings/Expenses/Attendance):
- Use the **Filter Bar** to select a Date Range (e.g., Jan 1 - Jan 31).
- Click **Filter**.
- Click **Export CSV** to download an Excel-compatible file.
- Click **Print View** for a printer-friendly version.

---

## 3. Church Administration

### Attendance
1. Go to **Attendance**.
2. Click **+ Record Attendance**.
3. Select **Service Type** (Sunday Service, etc.) and **Date**.
4. Enter counts for **Men**, **Women**, and **Children**.
5. Click **Record**.

### Evangelism Reports
1. Go to **Evangelism**.
2. Click **+ Submit Report**.
3. Enter stats for **Converts**, **Baptisms**, **New Members**.
4. Add counts for Discipleship activities.
5. Click **Submit**.

---

## 4. HR & Workers (Admin/Archid)

### Managing Workers
1. Go to **Workers**.
2. Click **+ Add Worker**.
3. Enter personal details and **Deployment Date**.
4. Set **Retirement Age** (auto-calculates years remaining).

### Contracts
- The system will alert you 90 days before a worker's contract expires.
- Check the **Notifications** bell icon at the top right.

---

## 5. System Settings (Boss Only)

### Managing Service Types
- Go to **Service Types** to add new events (e.g., "New Year Service").
- These will immediately appear in the Attendance form for all pastors.

### User Management
- Go to **Users** to create accounts for new Pastors or Archids.
- Assign the correct Role and Church to ensure data security.
